We have had the privilege and pleasure of working for incredible organisations, and with magnificent people tryingto leave the world a little better than they found it. Here’s just a small selection of projects we’ve been proud to work on:

  • Measuring the social and economic impact of a program to assist refugee and asylum seeker entrepreneurs start and grow viable small businesses

  • Creating an evaluation readiness toolkit for the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Child and Family Services sector

  • Developing a social impact measurement framework for a major energy provider to assess the impact of a suite of initiatives designed to support a suite of new and expanded initiatives

  •  Completing a developmental evaluation of a co-designed community health and wellbeing pilot with Asthma Australia to improve the wellbeing of the Arabic, Vietnamese and Samoan communities

  • Quantifying the social and economic value of the uberASSIST service to extend appropriate, accessible and reliable transport options for users with a disability or other accessibility needs

  • Evaluating the performance of an innovative project by the community legal assistance sector to strengthen collaboration and increase access to justice for communities
